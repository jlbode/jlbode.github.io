Humidity (%) vs. Latitude



The relationship between latitude and humidity is weak at best. Besides a higher share of cities with very high humidity near the equator, there is no distinct pattern elsewhere. Humidity and rain are driven by a number of other factors besides latitude; namely, how close the location is to water, hill and mountains (and which side the city is on), etc.
