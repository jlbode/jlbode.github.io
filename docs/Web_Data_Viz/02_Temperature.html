Temperature (F) vs. Latitude



Because the temperature depends on the time of day, it was more useful to plot the maximum temperature experienced at each location for the day. As expected, location further from equator, whether to the north or to the south, experienced cooler weather than locations near the equator. The hottest areas, however, are not exactly at the equator but north of it, reflecting the tilt of the earth at the time the sample temperature were drawn.
